Design thinking

Idea to Business needed a brand built from scratch that could speak directly to Hong Kong youth — energetic enough to excite them, credible enough to earn their trust. Starting from the founders' vision and core values, I translated their belief in action, curiosity, and companionship into a visual identity.

The identity is anchored in warm amber — a colour that communicates optimism and energy without feeling corporate. The logo combines a human profile, a lightbulb, and a growing tree — three symbols that together express the journey from idea to impact.
