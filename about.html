About Me

Hi there! I'm Mobie Chow — a brand designer who's spent years working inside NGOs, based in Hong Kong.

I started in branding design, working with social enterprises and NGOs from early on. Over time, I moved deeper into that world — managing volunteer programmes, facilitating cross-cultural workcamps, leading community projects. I didn't just design for these organisations. I worked inside them.

NGOs operate differently. Budgets are tighter. Stakeholders are more diverse. The materials you make will be used by volunteers who weren't in the briefing. I know this because I've been on both sides of the table — as the designer, and as the person running the programme.

If you're working on something that matters, let's figure it out together.
