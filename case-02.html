A Classroom Without Age
ClientWoopie Foundation
RoleIn-house
Nature
Brand Identity Social Enterprise

Project Brief

An intergenerational programme connecting seniors and children through collaborative art. Built the brand from scratch and brought it into physical space at H6 Content, Central.

Design thinking

The programme needed a visual identity that could communicate intergenerational connection without relying on words. I built the brand around two core ideas: colour as meaning, and form as story. The warm amber and slate palette blends warm and cool tones — a visual metaphor for what the programme creates when bringing generations together.

The logo uses negative space to reveal a larger hand, the elder's, holding a smaller one, the child's — making the brand's entire mission legible in a single mark. The identity was then brought into physical space at a 3-day exhibition at H6 Content, Central, where I designed the full layout and foam board displays.
